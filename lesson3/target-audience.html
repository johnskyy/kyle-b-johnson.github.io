Target Audience

"Personas"

Persona 1

Jim is a farmer and wants to have an easily accessible and easy to use site to check the local weather for his ranch in Preston. He is likely to use the site from a mobile device. He is getting on in years, and doesn't always have his glasses with him. As a result, he needs a site that can easily scale and is easy to read.

Persona 2

Tim works in Logan but has a cabin outside of Fish Haven. He often has the weather at the cabin pulled up on his computer. He appreciates a site that is easy to use and has clean design. He likes to check the weather to be able to know the best weeks/weekends to take family up to the cabin, or which weeks he should have it available on Airbnb.

  1. What's the weather going to be like in Preston/Fish Haven/Soda Springs?
  2. What did the hail stones look like that fell around Bear Lake?
  3. Is my child's school closed because of the weather?
  4. How long is this storm going to last?
  5. Is the fishing good at Bear Lake right now?
